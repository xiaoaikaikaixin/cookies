# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
This site serves Malaysia-based festive shoppers and business gift buyers looking for premium Chinese New Year cookies. They usually browse on mobile first, compare gift-worthiness and flavors quickly, and want a homepage that feels trustworthy, festive, and polished before they commit to purchase.

## Product Purpose
Present Lisa Handmade Delights as a warm, traditional, and refined CNY cookie brand, while guiding visitors into product discovery and purchase. Success means the homepage quickly communicates festive quality, signature products, gift-box appeal, and enough trust signals for both family orders and corporate gifting.

## Brand Personality
Warm, traditional, refined. The voice should feel festive and welcoming, with premium gift energy rather than loud promotion. It should balance Chinese New Year warmth with polished presentation and clear e-commerce actions.

## Anti-references
Do not make the homepage feel like a SaaS landing page, a generic dashboard, or a cold minimal boutique site with no festive warmth. Avoid cheap red-and-gold overload, cluttered promotion-page styling, and layouts that drift away from the uploaded homepage reference's composition and soft cream-gold tone.

## Design Principles
- Lead with festive trust: the first screen should immediately show brand, seasonality, signature products, and purchase intent.
- Make gifting feel elevated: product presentation should feel suitable for both family celebration and business gifting.
- Keep the journey obvious: navigation, category entry points, and shopping CTAs should be visible without visual noise.
- Use warmth with restraint: preserve seasonal richness through imagery, motifs, and palette without tipping into bargain-page intensity.
- Prioritize readable commerce: strong contrast, clear pricing, and mobile-friendly tap targets come before decorative flourish.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Target WCAG AA readability with strong text contrast on cream backgrounds, clear button states, and mobile-friendly spacing. Keep the homepage easy to scan for bilingual audiences, avoid overly aggressive motion, and maintain comfortable reading sizes for a broad age range.
